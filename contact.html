Why Graphic Design?

Originally, I chose graphic design out of pure instinct. I had been accepted into multiple programs and I just had a feeling that graphic design would work out for me, and it certainly has. I have fallen in love with the craft and I can't wait to use my skills from this program in my career going forwards.

Why Graphic Design?

Originally, I chose graphic design out of pure instinct. I had been accepted into multiple programs and I just had a feeling that graphic design would work out for me, and it certainly has. I have fallen in love with the craft and I can't wait to use my skills from this program in my career going forwards.

Long-Term Goals

Knowledge of Softwares

In the future, I want to master my softwares. Learning hotkeys and new tools makes me so excited about this field of work, and I want to be an efficient and skilled designer as I progress. I also want to learn brand new softwares, like Adobe Lightroom and After Effects.

Creating my Career

Once I'm graduated from my program, I want to build a strong client-base. I'd like to work with a variety of clients, like small businesses, large/well-established companies, and individual people to help with their own graphic design projects (like logos, branding and websites).

Owning a Home

Ultimately, my biggest goal is to be a homeowner. I want to make enough money in this industry to live comfortably, and have my own place. Having a designing space in the future is very important to me, even if that happens to a chaotic part of my kitchen.
