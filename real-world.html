My First Project

Apricity Guitars Canada Inc.

My first real-world, graphic design project was for Apricity Guitars Canada, a company founded by my dad, Andy. He is recently retired military, and decided to start a company building guitars, to raise money for the 13 children's hospitals in Canada. I designed Apricity's logo, business cards and sticker (to place inside the sound-hole of the guitars).

Graphic Design Services

Drawing/Sketching

I love to draw, but lately, I've found myself sketching out ideas/layouts instead of drawing a finished image. I think I prefer to base all of my projects off of a sketch, and build from there.

Typography

Since beginning to study graphic design, I've had to learn the rules of typography. The way we style text and use different typefaces can ultimately change the entire feel of a project.

Multi-element Docs

I love working on projects that include different mediums of graphic design. Designing mutli-element documents means using text, graphics, photography, etc. to create a project.

Web Developing

Web-developing has quickly become my favourite form of designing. I built this website myself! I LOVE coding, and styling things using classes/math makes me feel really smart and skilled in my craft.
